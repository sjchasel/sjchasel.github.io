    Title-Guided Encoding for Keyphrase Generation

    Research Objective

    pay more attention to the leading role of title when generating keyphrase

    Problem Statement

    In traditional methods for keyphrase prediction, the document title and the document main body are treated equally. But actually, title has a leading role to the whole document. Exsiting methods consequently fail to sufficiently utilize the already summarized information in titles.

    2. present keyphrase prediction task:CopyRNN and CopyCNN
    • evaluation metric:recall and F-measure (F1)
